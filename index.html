About Us

Hi! We're happy you've found us. We’re a 100% volunteer-driven organization, completely independent of the City of Pasadena. We’re DIY and have only a miniscule budget, yet we manage to get a lot done!

Our purpose is to promote Complete Streets in Pasadena, and we focus on projects and policies that support active transportation, such as walking, biking, and transit. We believe this enhances people’s health, promotes a sustainable planet, creates community and contributes to the local economy.

The best part about the Coalition is that we can do whatever the group wants! Want to change a policy, advocate for an issue, create a program, drop some street art? Let’s do it!
